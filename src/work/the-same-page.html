The Same Page

The Same Page is my newsletter project — where I write about creativity and leadership.

I was frustrated with the hamster-wheel of writing content to share consistently, daily. I needed to make it meaningfully fun to get myself back into the groove. I decided to go longform and physical. And I just love newsprint.

As a conversation starter, whipping out a newspaper instead of a business card is underrated. It's memorable, meaningful, and has more longevity than a LinkedIn post.

I'm inviting guest contributors and have visions of it becoming a larger collaborative project over time. Right now, it's a fun design exercise alongside a way to write focused content on the topics that interest me and my direct clients.
